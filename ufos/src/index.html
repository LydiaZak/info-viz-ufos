Visualizing UFO Sightings

An interactive exploration of UFO Sightings in the U.S. between 1910-2014

Abby S., Emily D., Lydia Z., Peter B. - CS 7295 April, 2018

The first (well) known UFO sighting was in 1947. While flying his small plane, Kenneth Arnold, saw a group of nine high-speed objects near Mount Rainier in Washington. He estimated the speed of the saucer-shaped objects as several thousand miles per hour. Since then, hundreds of thousands of UFO sightings have been submitted as possible proof of extraterrestrial evidence.\n The dataset that is used is from the National UFO Research Center. The dataset contains over 80,000 reports of UFO sightings over the last century, and it was geolocated and time standardized by Sigmond Axel. The data contains city, state, time, description, and duration of each sighting and gives detailed descriptions of each sighting by Peter Davenport, the Director of the National UFO Reporting Center in Washington State.
